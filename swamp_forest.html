Sylhet: The City of tea

Ratargul Swamp Forest

Ratargul is Bangladesh's only Swamp Forest. It is located about 26km outside of Sylhet district city. The Ratargul forest is spread over an area of approximately 30,325 acres. The 504-acre site is full of forests, and the rest of the area is complete with small and big water bodies. Ratargul is known as a “Sundarbans of Sylhet”. For four to five months out of the year, water completely covers the ground.
